THE O HOTEL GOA – BEST LUXURY BEACH RESORT AT CANDOLIM BEACH IN NORTH GOA NEAR THE BEACH

Our 5 star hotel, resort and spa is located right, 450 m from Candolim Beach North Goa. Our luxury hotel offers rooms with views of the sea, healing spa rituals and 3 distinct dining venues. The picturesque settings at our classic 5 star resort with a promise to soak in a space that showcases the spirit of Goa with its colonial Portuguese and Indian blend of cultures reflecting in our eclectic furnishings and Interiors, creates a fantastic backdrop for your entire experience with our elegant Indo-Portuguese architecture having high ceiling with stone pillars, larger than life chandeliers in The Goan Portuguese restaurant or at our beach side pool bar. Explore the horizon as you look over the waters of their Infinity pool and watch the tide as it works its way across the Arabian Sea. Expect the best in our Goan ,Portuguese & Indian cuisine, showcasing the master craftsmanship of our seasoned Chefs who would tease your palate with their epicurean delight.

LOCATION:Overlooking the Arabian Sea and surrounded by lush greenery of swaying palms, O Hotel Goa is a peaceful beach retreat near Candolim Beach (450 m) in North Goa.Located on Aguada - Siolim Road, the resort is 45 minutes from Thivim Railway Station (22 km) and about an hour from Goa International Airport (35 km). Tourist spots like Sinquerium Beach (1 km), Aguada Fort (4 km) and Dr. Salim Ali Bird Sanctuary (11 km) are easily accessible from here.
